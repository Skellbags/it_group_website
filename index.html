Location:

TRH Design provides a full range of Architectural services in Maine, New Hampshire, and Vermont. From concept through construction, TRH Design has the capabilities to tailor our services to your needs. The principal architect and founder of TRH Design is a LEED accredited professional. The firm’s design experience includes residential, community, institutional and commercial projects. As a small company, TRH Design remains intimately aware of all project details in an efficient manner, translating into a very competitive fee and personal attention to your project.

Additionally, one of our skills is the capability to provide a computer visualization of the project during the design process. It is advantageous because it allows you to modify the design, materials, and colors easily during the design process to avoid costly changes during construction. Upon completion of the design, a fully rendered and detailed image of the proposed project can provide owners and / or potential buyers with a true representation of the project before construction begins.

What makes TRH Design stand out from the competition?

Service:

Architectural design and planning: TRH Design provides architectural services from concept through construction. The architectural design process is broken into the following phases: schematic design, design development, construction documents, bidding, and administration of the construction. General concepts are explored during schematic design. We explore various solutions during this phase and upon completion of the schematic design, one solution is selected to be refined. During the design development phase, we describe the specific character and intent of the entire project.

Following the approval of the design, we develop construction documents and complete the bidding process to create final estimates with the contractor and team. The final phase of the design process is construction administration – TRH Design works closely with the team during this phase to ensure a smooth construction process is realized.

Construction Consultation: TRH Design works as a member of the team in this capacity. It is paramount in entering a contract that everything be as clear as possible. Oftentimes a contract is drafted without many items being addressed, leading to costly changes during construction. If these items are planned for prior to construction, the changes can be anticipated or avoided all together. TRH Design provides consultation as an independent entity to help ensure your project goes as smoothly as possible.

Building Analysis: TRH Design provides a full range of building analysis and consultation services. More specifically, we can offer the following services:

  • Spatial analysis and preliminary planning services: These services allow the owner to make informed decisions very early in the process.

  • Building envelope analysis: Using the integrated energy design approach, our design team provides options for lowering building operating costs while improving workers’ comfort and boosting productivity.

  • Building Code analysis: We advise and assist you in interpreting the applicable building codes and planning the best course of action to achieve a conforming project.

Computer Visualization: We are unique in our ability to create realistic computer visualizations of your project during the design process. This service is advantageous because it allows you to modify the design, materials, and colors easily during the design process to avoid costly changes during construction. Upon design completion, a fully rendered and detailed image of the proposed project can provide owners and/or potential buyers with a realistic representation of the project before construction begins.

Approach:

Design Collaboration & Style: Design and construction is inherently an exciting process. TRH Design does not take the position that any particular “style” is the one that has to be. The style that we subscribe to is a true collaboration with the owner and team. Some of the other factors that shape the design are context, environment, and local zoning. The collaboration translates into a project in which the owner is truly a part of and a building that embodies their vision, we make this vision become a reality. There is nothing more rewarding than that.

Team approach to the design/build process: A fundamental philosophy of TRH Design is collaboration from design through construction with all the team members (the owner, engineers, construction team and other consultants). Communication between these members is a key to a successfully coordinated, budgeted and timely project.

Small firm advantages: Being a small firm has many distinct advantages over larger firms. The first one being, there is one point of contact for all the design and construction decisions, eliminating any need for redundant communication within the office and team. Secondly, the efficiency in the office structure allows for a very competitive fee versus a larger firm, translating into a savings passed on to the owner. Computers have enabled efficiencies to be realized in all arenas of the design and construction processes, TRH Design is on the forefront of this. Finally, a small firm has the advantage that the owner is communicating directly with the person responsible for making decisions within the firm.

Experience & Thorough documents: In addition to 10 years of experience in a variety of project types in Architecture (residential, commercial and institutional), Todd Harrington has experience in construction management and civil engineering. This is advantageous because he has a thorough understanding about site design, the construction processes and the many important facets involved in coordinating and implementing these components.It is paramount that construction documents be thoroughly developed, coordinated and complete. Too often changes need to be made in the field. It is easier to make these changes on the drawings rather than in the field. During the construction coordination process, TRH design also uses overlays of the CAD files as a tool to facilitate the coordination of what is planned for the project (i.e. structure, mechanical, plumbing, etc.).
